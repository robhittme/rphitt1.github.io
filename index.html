Welcome Fellow Traveler.




My name is Robert P. Hitt and I am a front end engineer. I have always had a love for working with others to achieve great things and web development allows me to do just that. Through my education and various experiences, I have found the thing I enjoy most is working with others to solve problems that enrich lives. Since July of 2014 I've had the privilege to work at Qonceptual Inc. I Learned what it takes to be a team player, working closely with clients, and understanding the true meaning of an all nighter. I graduated from the Iron Yard in 2014, an intensive three month coding bootcamp that takes you from zero to front end engineer, and the I have not let the intensity dwindle. I have the confidence to overcome any problem that comes my way. Working with the amazing and talented people at Qonceptual, I have developed a passion for life-long learning, the art of coding, and being a part of this great community. If you want to check out my resume you can see some of the awesome stuff I can do.

I look forward to opportunities that allow me to work on rewarding projects with great people.

Projects

EZ Waves is a platform to book all types of recreational boating trips from Deep Sea Fishing excursions to Paddle boarding. Working with developers at Qonceptual, I took lead on the front-end development using AngularJS, working on the project from start to finish.

Focal was my final project at The Iron Yard. Focal is an App that provides information in a clear & concise manner to help inform people about the current state of affairs of their interest. Using AngularJS and Firebase to host, the user has the ability to search topics, with all the information in one place and a timeline to see how the issues have evolved.

Lead Zombie is a fun little app I made working with constructors. I created a game that created instances through the users choices. Be careful not to lose against the zombie, or you will become one too!
