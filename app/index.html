Welcome Fellow Traveller.

My name is Robert P. Hitt and I am a front end web developer. I have always had a love for working with others to achieve great things and web development allows me to do just that. Through my education and various experiences, I have found the thing I enjoy most is working with others to solve problems that enrich lives. I recently graduated from the Iron Yard, an intensive three month coding bootcamp that takes you from zero to front end engineer. Not only did the Iron Yard teach me how to build web applications from the ground up, but how to think like an engineer and tackle any problems that I encounter. I have the confidence to overcome any problem that comes my way. Working with the amazing staff and students at the Iron Yard, I have developed a passion for life-long learning, the art of coding, and being a part of this great community. If you want to check out my resume see some of the awesome stuff I can do.

I look forward to opportunities that allow me to work on awesome projects with awesome people.
